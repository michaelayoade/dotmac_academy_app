# Dotmac Academy — Nano Banana (Gemini image) prompt set

Brand: warm **sand/cream** backgrounds, **emerald** primary, **clay/terracotta**
accent. Editorial, human, technical-but-warm. Audience: Nigerian ISP field
engineers. Generate at the listed aspect ratio, export PNG, then it flows through
the app's WebP pipeline automatically.

**Shared style suffix** (append to every prompt):
> Flat editorial vector illustration, warm cream background (#faf6ee), emerald
> (#2f7a52) and terracotta (#c2683c) accents, clean geometric shapes, soft
> shadows, subtle paper grain, confident negative space, NO text, NO words, NO
> logos, calm and premium. Nigerian context where people appear.

> ⚠️ Decorative/branding only. Do NOT use AI for the 236 technical teaching
> figures (subnetting, topologies, addressing) — those stay authored for accuracy.
